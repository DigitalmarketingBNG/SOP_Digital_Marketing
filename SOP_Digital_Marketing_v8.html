Digital Marketing
Standard Operating Procedure

How we run and onboard campaigns.

Purpose

This document exists to give everyone a smooth, shared framework for running digital campaigns at Black n Green. It covers how we set campaign goals, get budgets approved, allocate spend month-by-month, and keep performance better.

📊
Keep budgets on track and well-managed
🎯
Maintain strong CPA and revenue efficiency
📈
Enable smart, structured scaling decisions
🛡️
Prevent financial and compliance risks

Who This Applies To

All Account Managers
Digital Marketing Team
Business Heads
This SOP covers all digital campaigns we run — whether through affiliates, DSPs, Google, or social media platforms.
01
Test Budget
  • Every new campaign — whether on affiliates, DSPs, Google, or social media — gets a test budget of up to USD 500.
  • This test budget can be used right away — no prior approval needed.
  • The test phase is there to help us understand:
    • Whether the traffic is good quality
    • How many users are converting (Conversion Rate)
    • What it's costing us to acquire each user (CPA)
02
Budget Allocation & Approval
  • Every campaign — new or ongoing — needs a formal budget approval from Rahul Gupta or Business Heads before spending beyond the test phase.
  • Once approved, the budget must be clearly mentioned.
  • Every budget plan should clearly mention:
    • Daily spending cap
    • CPA rates (provided by the Digital Team)
    • Total approved spend for the agreed period
03
Scaling & Extending Campaigns
  • Once a campaign has used its approved budget, the campaign will be paused until fresh approval is received from Rahul Gupta or Business Heads.
04
CPA Performance & Optimization
  • The Digital Team proposes initial CPA rates — these must be logged in the POD before any campaign goes live.
  • Expected CPA rates should be provided by the Account Managers.
  • CPA will be the metric on which the Digital Team will decide whether to scale, pause, or stop the campaign.
  • The Digital Team will try to optimize the CPA as low as possible within their operational capabilities.
05
Caps & Spend Management
  • The Digital Team must always stay within the total approved budget.
  • The Digital Team must always stay within the agreed daily caps.
  • If the daily caps are not met or the daily spend is falling short, the Digital Team will update the Account Manager and Business Head via Asana stating the reason behind the same.
06
Traffic Quality & Compliance
  • The Digital Team will only operate on approved GEOs, operators, and services.
  • The Digital Team will try to align the traffic quality to the best of their operational capabilities.
  • Any partners suggested by the Account Managers or Business Head — the Digital Team will run the campaign with that partner as per their guidance and will act only as facilitators, and shall not own the responsibility of that partner running the campaign.
07
POD — Promotion Onboarding Document
  • No campaign will be launched by the Digital Team without a fully completed POD.
  • The Account Manager is responsible for filling in all mandatory POD details — including a documented business case.
  • Business Heads must approve the POD before any campaign goes live.
  • The accuracy of everything in the POD is the Business Head's responsibility.
08
Review & Governance
  • Campaigns are reviewed regularly based on CPA, CPM and revenue performance.
  • If anyone needs to deviate from this SOP, prior written approval must be obtained from Pushpendra or Rahul Gupta —
    otherwise the campaign will not be executed.
